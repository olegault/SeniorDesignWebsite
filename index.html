CODE V WADE

Take control of your reproductive health privacy

We are a platform that ranks how well reproductive health apps (such as fertility applications) protect your privacy. We provide each app a privacy score from 0 to 100, which we calculate based on a mixed-methods analysis of the application's data protection. Our score is accompanied by analysis of what specifically the application does with user data. All this information is available at our website, where you can search through our database opf rankings or submit a new app to be scored.
